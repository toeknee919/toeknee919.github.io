About Us

Blue Barn Wagyu is a small local Waygu Ranch which raises and sells Wagyu cattle. Waygu, also known as the "American Style Kobe Beef" is a high quality beef sold in most high end restaurants. We raise both full blood Waygu and some mixed with high percentages of Wagyu usually in the 80-90 percent range. Please contact us if you are seriously interested in purchasing one of our cattle.

Healthy and Grass Fed

Our Wagyu are grass fed and raised on a healthy farm here in Brownsville Oregon located between Albany and Eugene East of Interstate 5.

Lots of Space to Roam

Our small herd of Wagyu have 10 acres to roam with fresh green grass to eat almost year round.

Clean Cows Taste Better

Our property is well drained which keeps our cows out of the mud and provides healthy fresh grass for them to eat.
